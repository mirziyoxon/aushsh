About The Unicorner Times

The Unicorner Times is a student-driven journal that explores global economics, culture, and politics with clarity and depth. Founded by young thinkers from Uzbekistan, we blend modern journalism with intellectual curiosity — inspired by the likes of The New York Times and The New Yorker.

“We believe good journalism doesn’t shout — it whispers truth with conviction.”

Our stories are powered by diverse perspectives, crafted with precision, and published with passion. The Unicorner Times stands as a platform for emerging voices in global discourse — connecting creativity, credibility, and community.
