About The Unicorner Times

Founded by a new generation of thinkers, The Unicorner Times reimagines how Central Asia reads, learns, and creates — bridging journalism, research, and storytelling with global perspective.

Our Community

Unicorner connects students, writers, and innovators across more than 20 countries. Together, they share the conviction that curiosity and collaboration are stronger than borders. We publish in multiple languages and mediums, supporting independent youth voices across the region.

Our Mission

We believe knowledge is a public good. Our mission is to make research readable, stories relatable, and ideas reachable — creating an open space where insight meets imagination. Every publication, whether analytical or poetic, contributes to a growing archive of thought for the future.
